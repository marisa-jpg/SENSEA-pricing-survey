# SENSEA Survey Quick Reference

## Survey Overview

**Purpose:** Collect comprehensive feedback for SENSEA's 2025 strategic planning

**Target Audience:** SENSEA customers (current and potential)

**Estimated Completion Time:** 8-12 minutes

**Total Questions:** 38 questions across 8 sections

## Survey Sections Summary

### 1. Spa Day Pass Pricing Review (4 questions)
**Goal:** Understand customer price perception and willingness to pay
- Current pricing satisfaction
- Purchase likelihood
- Ideal price range
- Open feedback

### 2. New Membership Offering Test (5 questions)
**Goal:** Assess membership program viability and preferences
- Interest level
- Preferred membership types
- Expected benefits
- Price sensitivity
- Additional thoughts

### 3. Social and Ritual Events (5 questions)
**Goal:** Evaluate event attendance and preferences
- Past attendance
- Event satisfaction
- Desired event types
- Attendance frequency
- Open feedback

### 4. Spa Opening Hours Review - Off-Peak (5 questions)
**Goal:** Optimize scheduling and identify off-peak opportunities
- Current hours satisfaction
- Off-peak discount interest
- Preferred time slots
- Discount thresholds
- Additional feedback

### 5. Loyalty Program (5 questions)
**Goal:** Design effective loyalty program features
- Importance assessment
- Preferred rewards
- Tracking preferences
- Recommendation likelihood
- Program thoughts

### 6. Treatments and Additional Offerings (5 questions)
**Goal:** Optimize treatment menu and service variety
- Past treatment experience
- Quality ratings
- Desired new treatments
- Variety satisfaction
- Enhancement suggestions

### 7. Areas for Improvement, Final Thoughts (6 questions)
**Goal:** Identify improvement priorities and overall satisfaction
- Improvement areas
- Overall experience rating (1-10)
- Net Promoter Score
- Favorite aspects
- Visit frequency drivers
- Additional comments

### 8. Demographics (3 questions - Optional)
**Goal:** Segment feedback for targeted improvements
- Age range
- Visit frequency
- Discovery channel
